  1. Prepare and enjoy dinner at 7:00 PM.

  1. Engage in a hobby or personal project from 8:00 PM to 9:30 PM.
  1. Wind down with a 15-minute bedtime meditation.
  1. Go to bed at 10:00 PM for a good night's sleep.
